# What this game is

Don't Touch Prod is a game about a small IT team that receives business work every day and tries to ship it to production without creating chaos.

You do not write code directly. You manage the flow of work:

- choose which tasks to take;
- decide who should work on them;
- watch deadlines;
- decide when a task is good enough to ship;
- deal with the consequences of weak decisions.

The goal is not to make everything perfect. Resources are limited. The core skill is deciding where to take risk and where to clean things up.

## Main idea

Every task can help the business: value, clients, trust, or lower debt. But if you ship it dirty, it can break something tomorrow.

Good play is not "do everything as fast as possible". Good play is choosing which problem matters most right now.
